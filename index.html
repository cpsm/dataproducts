Introduction

  • Data from 1974 Motor Trend US Magazine to compare 10 aspects of automoile design and performance is stored in mtcars data

    • Data consists of design and pertormance of 32 automobile during the year(1973-74)
    • The variables are as follows 1 Miles/(US) gallon 2 Number of cylinder 3 Displacement (cu.in.) 4 Gross horsepower 5 Rear axle ratio 6 Weight (lb/1000) 7 1/4 mile time 8 V/S 9 Transmission (0 = automatic, 1 = manual) 10 Number of forward gears 11 Number of carburetors
